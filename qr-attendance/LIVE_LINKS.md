## ⚡ What to do next
1. Open the **Google Sheet** link above.
2. Go to the menu at the top: **`QR Attendance` → `Initialize System`**
   *(It will ask for permissions since it's the first time running. Click Review Permissions → Choose your account → Advanced → Go to QR Attendance)*
3. Once initialized, go to the menu again: **`QR Attendance` → `Generate All QR Codes`**
4. Open the **Scanner Web App** link on your phone.
5. Scan one of the QR codes generated in your Google Drive folder (`QR_Attendance_Codes`).
6. Watch the Google Sheet `Logs` tab update in real-time!
